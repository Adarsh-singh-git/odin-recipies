Panner-Masala

Description :

The traditional paneer masala recipe is a healthy combination of paneer chunks, dry groceries, fruits, and vegetables along with the right quantity of spices. It takes nearly 20-30 minutes to prepare the dish and makes it an easy and simple meal for your family for a weekend treat.

Ingredients :

  • Panner
  • Onion , tomatos , and Masalas
  • Little bit water
  • Things you want to garnish with

Steps :

  1. Wash paneer with warm water.
  2. Put a oil let it warm , put masala paste and cook until it's totally cooked
  3. Put Panner
  4. Let it cook for few minutes
  5. Stop the flame and you are ready to Serve
